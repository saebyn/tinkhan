Tin Khan allows you to bring the experiences of your organization's learners from Khan Academy into your organization's Tin Can capable learning record store.

Khan Academy activities such as watching videos, practicing on exercises, and earning badges are converted into Tin Can statements that are sent to your organization's LRS.
